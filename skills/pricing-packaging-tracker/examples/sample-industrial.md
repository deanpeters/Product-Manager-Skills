## Why this example works

- **Zero prices, real intelligence.** Every finding is structural — an edition split, a gate
  placement, a pilot program dying, a waitlist appearing. The tracker's "structure over numbers"
  teaching isn't a fallback here; it's the entire method, because this market publishes nothing
  else.
- **The do-not-invent rule got stress-tested and held.** The tempting move — estimating Foresight
  Plus pricing from service-contract anecdotes — is exactly what the report refuses; the price
  *posture* inference is labeled and number-free.
- **Cross-channel corroboration on Meridian:** a registry filing (watch report) plus a demand-side
  signup form (this capture) is two independent channels telling one entry story — the stacking
  rule operating across skills, not just within one.
- **The Signals section turned the mirror around** — the market's opacity makes Northfield's
  transparency strategically loud, a question this capture surfaces and routes to the pricing
  advisor rather than answering by vibe.
