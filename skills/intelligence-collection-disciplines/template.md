# Collection Plan Template

Copy this to plan a collection effort before running any discipline. The plan turns the compendium
from a course into an operation: fill the six instantiation variables, pick the two or three
disciplines that feed your decision (never all eight), and set the cadence to how fast each
evidence channel actually changes. **If [DECISION] is blank, stop — research without a decision is
a hobby.**

## Template

```markdown
# Collection Plan: [TARGET] / [CAPABILITY]

## Instantiation
[TARGET]      = [the competitor, partner, or acquirer]
[MARKET]      = [category + NAICS/SIC/NACE codes or nearest equivalent]
[GEOGRAPHY]   = [regions in scope, at COUNTRY level]
[BUYER]       = [who signs the check]
[CAPABILITY]  = [the strategic move you suspect]
[DECISION]    = [what this research will change — REQUIRED]
```

## Quality checks before you call it done

- [DECISION] is filled in and specific — it selects the disciplines and defines "enough"
- Two or three disciplines are marked Running, with chain-level reasons — eight checkmarks means no plan
- The fusion verdict uses the stacking rule (1 = watch, 2 = hypothesis, 3+ = actionable, conflict = dig)
- Announcements in the fusion table are labeled intent until FININT/MASINT/HUMINT corroborate
